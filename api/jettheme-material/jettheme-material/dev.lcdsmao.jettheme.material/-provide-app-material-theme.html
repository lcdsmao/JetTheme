Theme material design version of ProvideAppTheme. A convenient version of ProvideMaterialTheme suited to provide global app theme. Configure the ThemeController by ThemeConfig.Persistence, so theme preference will be persisted in the local storage.

Parameters

themePack

the ThemePack to construct a ThemeConfig.Persistence
